---
title: "Custom team invites"
description: "Integrate Village with your Team Management feature and allow your users to easily share their networks."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customize your section" titleSize="h2">
    Go to the [Platform Admin](https://village.do/platform/settings/features) settings and customize the team sections that will appear on your user's Sync Modal

    ![SCR 20250325 Mtsv Pn](/images/SCR-20250325-mtsv.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to your integration" stepNumber="2" titleSize="h2">
    Village.identify 2nd parameter is a config object, you can pass "team" with an id and/or name and we'll handle the rest.

    ```javascript
    Village.identify(USER_ID, {
      team: {
        id: TEAM_ID,
        name: TEAM_NAME,
      },
    });
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
