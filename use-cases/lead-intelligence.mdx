## Lead intelligence platforms key challenges

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Low lead conversion rates" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Sales teams waste time on unqualified or cold leads, resulting in poor conversion rates and low ROI on outreach efforts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Missing out on identifying warm leads" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Without visibility into shared networks or mutual connections, reps miss opportunities to build trusted connections utilizing their network reputation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Inefficient lead prioritization" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Platforms lack tools to rank leads based on trustworthiness or network strength, leading to inefficiencies in resource allocation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How Village helps you address these challenges

  </Accordion>
