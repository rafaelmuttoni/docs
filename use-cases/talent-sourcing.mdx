## Talent sourcing platforms key challenges

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Inefficient candidate discovery" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Recruiters struggle to find high-quality candidates quickly, especially for niche or hard-to-fill roles.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Limited network insights" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Platforms don’t provide tools to leverage shared networks or mutual connections for discovering top talent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Low engagement with passive talent" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Passive candidates often ignore cold outreach due to a lack of trust or personalization.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
