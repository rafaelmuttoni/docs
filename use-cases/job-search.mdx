## Job search platforms key challenges

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cold job applications" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Job seekers face low response rates from employers due to the lack of warm connections, making the process frustrating and inefficient.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lack of personalized job recommendations" defaultOpen={false} icon="face-frown">
    Platforms often fail to match users with roles based on their networks, reducing the relevance of job recommendations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
